Our Story

Welcome to FeastFlick! We started with a simple mission: to make delicious food accessible to everyone, anytime, anywhere. Created in 2025, we've grown from a small local service to a platform that connects food lovers with their favorite restaurants across the city.

Our team is passionate about food and technology, working tirelessly to ensure every order is delivered hot, fresh, and on time. We partner with local restaurants to bring you a diverse range of cuisines right to your doorstep.
